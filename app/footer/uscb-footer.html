Description

Purpose

The footer allows the user to navigate to other relevant links.

Usage

The footer appears at the bottom of the page and is persistent across all pages. The footer consists of text links that allow the user to navigate to other relevant links. These links include information on: Accessibility, Information Quality, Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), Data Protection and Privacy Policy, the U.S. Department of Commerce, and software Release Notes.

Do
  • Show the footer at the bottom of each page
  • Retain placement of footer and content of footer on each page
Do not
  • Make the footer fixed at the bottom of the current viewport
